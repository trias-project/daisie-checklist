3.17 taxonRank

Information for taxonRank is provided in the field subtaxon_rank, but is only given for varieties, aggregates, hybrids, subspecies or forms. Taxon rank information can also be retrieved by the GBIF nameparser function. This information was retrievd earlier in this script, in the dataframe parsed_names. We add the information to taxon.
